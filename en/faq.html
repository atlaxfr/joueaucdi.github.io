Before contacting us, you must first make sure that you are dealing with a real bug. To do this, talk to the community either casually by the forum or by discord or open a ticket to ask for help from a member of the team. This one will be able to confirm the bug and will be able to also guide you in writing your bug report.

The recommended model for writing a bug report is the step-by-step model which is as follows:
Name: Ex: Button not clickable
Severity: Low/Medium/High
Theme: Ex: LShadow
Occurrence time (Optional): Ex: At login
Open a ticket in your customer area or on discord detailing and arguing your ideas.
Interesting ideas are retained by our team and then transferred to an appropriate list on a site

Launcher

If you encounter a problem that you can't figure out about your launcher, you can open a ticket in the "support" section. from your customer area or on discord. Before opening a ticket, please respect the following rules:

Usually it comes from a configuration error in your modpack:

  • If you have a modpack and you go through the download method via a link, check that it is accessible and that your modpack is downloaded directly when you access it.
  • If you are not in this case, check that the files in your modpack are well placed in structured folders such as in the game directory. For example, mod files go in the "mods" folder in the root of your modpack.

it could also come from an error in addition. To solve your problem, get the launcher logs that you can find in the game directory > in the launcher folder (the launcher-log.txt file) and open a ticket on the online support.

Payment and refund

No refund can take place even for a perilous reason. So there is no need to contact us knowing that this is mentioned in our conditions of sale.
If you made a payment with paypal, your transaction may be temporarily blocked to be subject to verification.

We guarantee the availability of our services except in the event of a breakdown or malfunction. When an unavailability occurs, we mobilize all our forces to restore the services concerned.

In other words, if your service does not work because it is unavailable, know that you have to be patient during the time it takes to get back into service. Feel free to join our discord server to be informed. You also have a page at your disposal to follow the status of our services.

If however your problem is outside this scope and it concerns a configuration problem, we can provide you with technical assistance.

That said, no refund can be made regardless of the situation in which you are.
